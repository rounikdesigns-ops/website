Our Story

Born from a Love
of Beautiful Clothing

ROUNIK was founded by Ruchi and Niki — two friends who believed that clothing should feel personal, intentional, and made with heart. What started as a shared passion for design became a label celebrated for its craftsmanship and warmth.

The Founders

Meet Ruchi & Niki

Ruchi Jain
Co-Founder & Creative Director

Ruchi brings years of experience in fashion design to every piece she creates. With a deep understanding of fabrics and a keen eye for detail, she turns ideas into wearable art. Based in Mumbai, she leads the design and production at ROUNIK.

Niki
Co-Founder & Style Curator

Niki is the creative force behind ROUNIK's aesthetic direction. She curates every collection with a vision of what modern Indian women want to wear — confident, elegant, and true to themselves. Her passion for style drives the brand forward.
